# Privacy Policy **Effective date: April 8, 2026** ## 1. Overview FXVault ("we", "our", "us") is a trading education and calculation app. This policy explains what data is processed when you use the app. ## 2. Data We Process ### A) Third-party SDK and infrastructure data **Google AdMob (ads)** If ads are enabled, AdMob may process advertising identifiers and device data for ad delivery and measurement. AdMob policy: **Firebase Analytics** We process product-analytics events (for example: screen views, feature usage, ad impression revenue events) to improve the app. **Firebase Crashlytics** We process crash and diagnostic data (for example: stack traces, device model, OS version, app version) to monitor and fix stability issues. **Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)** We process push tokens and messaging metadata to deliver notifications you enable. **Supabase backend** We process app backend data such as alert preferences, app-generated content/cache, and related operational records needed to provide features. ### B) Data stored on your device - Calculator inputs and history - Journal entries and notes - Local app preferences (language, settings, toggles) - Local notification history and reminder settings ### C) Account and purchases FXVault currently does **not** require account registration in-app and does **not** provide paid subscription purchases in-app at this time. ### D) Advertising identifiers If you have not opted out of ad personalization, AdMob may collect your Android Advertising ID (AAID) for ad delivery and measurement purposes. You can reset or opt out via: Android Settings → Google → Ads → Delete advertising ID ## 3. How We Use Data - Operate core app features - Deliver notifications you opted into - Improve performance, reliability, and UX - Measure ad performance and app health - Prevent abuse and maintain service security We do **not** sell your personal data. ## 4. Data Retention - Local device data remains until you clear app data or uninstall. - Backend records are retained as needed for service operation, security, and legal obligations. - Third-party SDK data retention follows each provider's policy. ## 5. Your Choices and Rights - Disable notifications in app settings or OS settings. - Reset/limit ad personalization in Android settings. - Request data deletion support at **support@fxvault.app**. - If applicable in your region (for example GDPR/CCPA), you may request access, correction, or deletion. **Data Deletion Request** To request deletion of your backend app data, email **support@fxvault.app** with subject line "Data Deletion Request". We will action your request within 30 days. You may also delete local data at any time by clearing app storage or uninstalling the app. ## 6. Children's Privacy FXVault is intended for adults and trading learners; it is not directed to children under the applicable minimum age. ## 7. Policy Changes We may update this policy periodically. Material updates will be reflected via app update and/or updated effective date. ## 8. Contact Questions or requests: **support@fxvault.app** --- *View the latest version online: [fxvault.app/privacy](https://fxvault.app/privacy)*
