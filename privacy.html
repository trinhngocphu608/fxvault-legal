Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 12, 2026

1. Overview

FXVault ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the FXVault mobile application ("App").

2. Information We Collect

Automatically Collected by Third-Party SDKs

Google AdMob (Advertising)
FXVault displays advertisements to free-tier users via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings under Google → Ads.
AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

RevenueCat (Subscription Management)
If you purchase FXVault Premium, RevenueCat processes your subscription status and purchase history. RevenueCat does not store your payment card details — all payments are handled by Google Play.
RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Data You Provide (Stored Locally Only)

The following data is stored exclusively on your device and is never transmitted to our servers:

  • Trading journal entries
  • Calculator history and inputs
  • App settings and preferences
  • Academy learning progress

We have no access to your personal trading records.

Market Data Requests

To display live prices and financial news, the App makes anonymous requests to our price server and third-party data providers (TwelveData, Finnhub). These requests do not include any personal identifying information.

3. How We Use Information

  • AdMob data: to display relevant advertisements
  • RevenueCat data: to manage your Premium subscription
  • We do not sell your data to any third party

4. Data Retention

  • Local app data: retained until you uninstall the App
  • AdMob/RevenueCat data: governed by their own policies

5. Children's Privacy

FXVault is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

6. Your Rights

  • Reset your Advertising ID via Android device settings
  • Delete all local app data by uninstalling the App
  • Cancel your subscription anytime via Google Play

7. Changes to This Policy

We will notify users of material changes via an app update. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of changes.
