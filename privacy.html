# Privacy Policy **Effective date: March 12, 2026** ## 1. Overview FXVault ("we", "our", "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information when you use the FXVault mobile application ("App"). ## 2. Information We Collect ### Automatically Collected by Third-Party Services **Google AdMob (Advertising)** FXVault displays advertisements to free-tier users via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect your device's Advertising ID to show relevant ads. You can reset or opt out of personalized ads in your Android device settings under Google → Ads. AdMob Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy **RevenueCat (Subscription Management)** If you purchase FXVault Premium, RevenueCat processes your subscription status and purchase history. RevenueCat does not store your payment card details — all payments are handled by Google Play. RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy **Crash Reporting Service** FXVault uses an automated crash reporting service to detect and fix app errors. When a crash occurs, technical information is collected including device model, OS version, app version, and error details. This data contains no personally identifiable information and is used solely to improve app stability. **Analytics Service** FXVault uses an analytics service to understand general usage patterns (e.g. which features are used most). All data is anonymized and aggregated. No individual user behavior is tracked or stored. **Push Notification Service** To deliver economic event alerts, FXVault uses a push notification service that assigns an anonymous device token to your device. This token is used only for notification delivery and is not linked to your identity. **Cloud Backend** FXVault uses a secure cloud backend to store: - Your alert preferences (currencies and timing settings you configure in the Alerts tab) - Cached market data and economic calendar information - AI-generated educational content No personal identity information (name, email, address, phone number) is stored on our servers. ### Data You Configure **Synced to our servers:** - Alert preferences (currencies, timing, quiet hours) — to enable push notifications for economic events **Stored exclusively on your device:** - Calculator history and inputs - App settings and display preferences - Reward balance ### AI-Generated Content FXVault uses AI processing services to generate: - Daily, weekly, and monthly market digest summaries - Educational explanations for economic indicators AI-generated content is created automatically for educational purposes only. It may contain inaccuracies and should never be used as the basis for trading decisions. Generated content is cached on our servers and is not personalized to individual users. ### Market Data Economic calendar data and price information displayed in this App is sourced from established third-party financial data providers. Data may be delayed or incomplete. FXVault does not guarantee the accuracy of any market data displayed. ### Data Deletion FXVault does not require account creation. To remove all locally stored data, uninstall the App. To remove your alert preferences from our servers, disable all alerts in the Alerts tab before uninstalling, or contact support@fxvault.app. ### Your Rights - Opt out of personalized ads via Android device settings - Delete local data by uninstalling the App - Request deletion of server-stored alert preferences by contacting support@fxvault.app - EU/EEA users have rights under GDPR including access, rectification, and erasure of personal data ## 3. How We Use Information - AdMob data: to display relevant advertisements - RevenueCat data: to manage your Premium subscription - Crash data: to diagnose and fix technical issues - Analytics data: to improve the App in aggregate - Push tokens: to deliver economic event alerts you enable - Cloud data: to sync alert preferences and serve cached content - We do **not** sell your data to any third party ## 4. Data Retention - Local app data: retained until you uninstall the App - Server-stored alert preferences: retained until you disable alerts, request deletion, or we remove them per policy - AdMob/RevenueCat data: governed by their own policies ## 5. Children's Privacy FXVault is intended for users aged 18 and older. We do not knowingly collect data from minors. ## 6. Changes to This Policy We will notify users of material changes via an app update. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of changes. ## 7.
